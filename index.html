About Us

D&D Groceries is a family-owned grocery store that provides customers with a wide range of items including rice, dal, flours, oil, sugar/jaggery, salt, spices, vegetables, tea/coffee, and snacks. We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality products and excellent customer service. Our goal is to make shopping for groceries as convenient and hassle-free as possible. Whether you're a busy professional or a busy parent, we've got you covered.
